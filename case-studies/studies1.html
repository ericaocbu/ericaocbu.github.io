Detecting Early Indicators of a Mental Health Crisis

Summary

Project Goal

To create a moderator-facing tool that visualizes mental health crisis indicators from Reddit posts, making complex text analytics more interpretable and actionable. The dashboard focuses on accessibility, clarity, and efficiency in identifying users at risk.

My Role

Lead UX/UI Designer and Developer. Responsible for user research with moderators, designing data visualizations, creating wireframes and interactive prototypes, and iterating through usability testing.

Duration

5 weeks (June 2025 - July 2025)

Project Timeline

Week 1: Research & Strategy

Conducted research on moderator workflows and pain points. Reviewed existing mental health monitoring tools and competitive dashboards. Defined project scope and success metrics with emphasis on early detection.

Week 2: Ideation & Brainstorming

Developed user personas (focused on moderators). Mapped user journeys to capture moderator needs and decision-making processes. Sketched initial dashboard layouts and explored visualization types (heatmaps, sentiment timelines, severity bars).

Week 3: Style Guide & UI Design

Established visual identity with emphasis on clarity and accessibility. Defined typography, color palette (including accessibility-compliant contrasts), and chart styles. Designed initial high-fidelity dashboard screens.

Week 4: Usability Testing

Conducted usability sessions with sample moderator personas. Gathered feedback on dashboard clarity, ease of interpretation, and navigation flow. Iterated on data visualization components for better readability.

Week 5: Finalization

Refined dashboard design based on feedback. Documented insights and finalized assets for developer handoff. Delivered an interactive prototype showcasing end-to-end moderator workflows.

Problem + Solution

The Problem

Moderators often struggle to interpret complex mental health signals in user-generated content. Existing tools can be overwhelming, overly technical, or lack the ability to synthesize multiple signals, delaying interventions and increasing moderator fatigue.

The Solution

The dashboard provides:

  • Integrated model-based and rule-based analysis for holistic insights.
  • Sentiment and linguistic trend visualization to highlight risk patterns.
  • Severity classification levels that are intuitive and actionable.
  • Clear, accessible design that supports rapid interpretation.
