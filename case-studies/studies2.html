Problem + Solution

The Problem

Students often struggle to navigate internship applications, portfolio creation, and resume development. They may lack guidance, structured workflows, and feedback, leading to stress and missed opportunities.

The Solution

InCompass provides:

  • Step-by-step portfolio guidance for projects, wireframes, and prototypes.
  • Internship tracking tools to manage applications, deadlines, and statuses.
  • Resume feedback forums for peer and mentor guidance.
  • Community support to share advice and resources.
  • Accessible and intuitive design for streamlined user experiences.
