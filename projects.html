Detecting Early Indicators of a Mental Health Crisis

I developed an interactive moderator-facing dashboard designed to visualize mental health severity classifications from Reddit posts. The dashboard integrates both model-based and rule-based outputs, displaying sentiment trends, key linguistic markers, and severity levels in an intuitive interface. By combining clear visualizations with underlying data insights, the tool supports quicker interpretation of complex text analytics, aiming to aid early detection and intervention for individuals in distress.

GentleCare

I designed a Mobile Health and Wellness App for Seniors with Vision Impairments, focusing on simplicity, accessibility, and ease of use. The app allows users to track daily steps, receive pill reminders, and log important health metrics like blood pressure, weight, and blood sugar. It also supports secure messaging and data sharing with healthcare providers, helping patients stay connected and monitored. To accommodate users with low vision or hearing, the design features high-contrast colors, large touch targets, clear typography, and optional speech assistance, ensuring that health management is accessible to all.
